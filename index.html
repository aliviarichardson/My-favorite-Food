Why I Love Alfredo

Alfredo is my absolute favorite comfort food. The creamy garlic sauce mixed with pasta is so rich and satisfying that it always makes me feel warm and relaxed.

I remember the first time I had Alfredo on June 10, 2018. Since then, it has become my go-to meal whenever I want something delicious that feels like a hug in a bowl.

Key Ingredients in My Alfredo

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Butter and heavy cream
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic, salt, and pepper
  • A little pasta water
  • Served with a cold glass of H2O
